Instructions

How To Start:

To start the game click "Start" in the game window.

Controls
Use the keyboard arrow keys to move around the game window
  • Right Arrow Key - Move your character right;
  • Left Arrow Key - Move your character Left;
  • Up Arrow Key - Move your character Up;
  • Down Arrow Key - Move your character Down;
  • F Key - Changes your avatar to a fireball that has a wider path. Moves as fast as your opponent.
  • S Key - Changes your avatar to a water dropplet to create a thinner line. Moves as fast as your opponent.
  • D Key - Changes your avatar to a lightning bolt. Moves as twice as fast as your opponent and 50% chance of passing through lines.
Game Play

Try to survive longer than your opponent. Don't hit any lines or the game window.
